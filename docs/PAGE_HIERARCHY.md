# Page Hierarchy

## Tier 1: Conversion-First Pages

- Home
- Product category pages
- Solution pages
- Contact page

## Tier 2: Trust-Building Pages

- About
- Projects
- FAQ
- Shipping and export information
- Quality and materials

## Tier 3: SEO and Thought Leadership Pages

- Journal index
- Journal articles
- Location or intent-specific landing pages

## Homepage Role

The homepage must act as a premium overview and navigation hub, not a generic landing page. It should quickly answer what the brand offers, who it serves, and why a visitor should trust it.

## Product Page Role

Each product page should support scan-friendly evaluation, showcase product context, and end with a clear enquiry route.

## Solution Page Role

Solution pages should translate product value into business outcomes for specific buyer types.

## Support Page Role

Support pages must eliminate procurement uncertainty and reinforce professionalism.
