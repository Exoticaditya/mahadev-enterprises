# Information Architecture

## IA Principles

- Organize by buyer intent before product volume.
- Prioritize business use cases over internal catalog structure.
- Keep primary navigation small and decisive.
- Make trust, products, and enquiry paths available within one click where possible.

## Content Layers

### Layer 1: Brand Understanding
Homepage, about, trust signals, and positioning.

### Layer 2: Product Discovery
Category pages, product collections, and use-case collections.

### Layer 3: Conversion
Enquiry forms, contact pathways, catalogue request, and consultation prompts.

### Layer 4: Proof and Support
Testimonials, project examples, capabilities, FAQs, and process explanation.

## Navigation Model

- Home
- Products
- Solutions
- About
- Insights or Journal
- Contact

## Structural Logic

Visitors should be able to enter from a search result on any product or solution page and still understand:

- Who Mahadev Enterprises is.
- What they supply.
- Who the products are for.
- How to enquire.
- Why the brand is trustworthy.

## Content Governance

Information should be written in a consistent tone: calm, confident, minimal, and commercially clear. Sections should be designed to reduce decision friction rather than create marketing noise.
