# 01 Colors

## Color Philosophy

The palette must feel calm, premium, and materially grounded. It should evoke stone, linen, clay, olive, and polished metal rather than bright retail colors. Color is a structural system here, not decoration.

## Primary Colors

- **Mahadev Ink** `#111111`: Primary text, dark mode base, major UI anchors.
- **Ivory Mist** `#F6F2EB`: Primary light background and surface tone.
- **Stone Beige** `#E6DED2`: Secondary surface tone for subtle section separation.

## Secondary Colors

- **Olive Ash** `#5E6653`: Secondary brand tone for wellness and nature association.
- **Sage Veil** `#BFC7B6`: Soft supporting tone for tags, chips, and low-emphasis panels.
- **Warm Taupe** `#A79A8A`: Supporting neutral for dividers and quiet UI accents.

## Accent Colors

- **Brass Accent** `#B08A57`: Primary accent for CTAs, highlights, and premium cues.
- **Terracotta Clay** `#B56A4A`: Secondary accent for editorial emphasis and careful contrast.
- **Deep Forest** `#1F3A2E`: Reserved accent for brand depth, trust, and selected states.

Accent colors must be used sparingly. The interface should never feel colorful; it should feel curated.

## Success

- **Success Deep** `#2F6B4F`
- **Success Soft** `#DCEADF`

Success should feel understated and credible, never neon.

## Warning

- **Warning Amber** `#B9842F`
- **Warning Soft** `#F3E5C4`

Warnings should be reserved for form states and procurement notices.

## Error

- **Error Deep** `#A8453E`
- **Error Soft** `#F4D8D5`

Errors should be legible, calm, and uncompromising on accessibility.

## Dark Mode Colors

- **Dark Background** `#0B0C0B`
- **Dark Surface** `#131514`
- **Dark Elevated Surface** `#1A1D1B`
- **Dark Border** `#2A2F2C`
- **Dark Text Primary** `#F2F0EB`
- **Dark Text Secondary** `#C6C1B8`
- **Dark Muted Accent** `#8D7C63`

Dark mode should feel like a gallery at dusk, not a reversed light theme.

## Glass Colors

Glass effects must remain restrained and highly readable.

- **Glass Light** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.62)`
- **Glass Dark** `rgba(17, 17, 17, 0.42)`
- **Glass Border Light** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.28)`
- **Glass Border Dark** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.12)`

Glass is allowed only for hero overlays, floating CTAs, and select nav states.

## Gradients

Gradients must be near-imperceptible and material, not decorative.

- **Ivory to Linen:** `#FAF7F3` to `#F1EAE0`
- **Stone to Mist:** `#E8E0D5` to `#F7F4EF`
- **Ink to Forest:** `#111111` to `#1F3A2E`
- **Brass Wash:** `#B08A57` to `#D7BC8A`

Use gradients only in large expanses or atmospheric overlays. Never use saturated or rainbow-like gradients.

## Shadows

Shadows should be quiet and architectural.

- **Shadow XS:** `0 1px 2px rgba(17, 17, 17, 0.06)`
- **Shadow SM:** `0 4px 12px rgba(17, 17, 17, 0.08)`
- **Shadow MD:** `0 10px 24px rgba(17, 17, 17, 0.10)`
- **Shadow LG:** `0 18px 40px rgba(17, 17, 17, 0.12)`
- **Shadow Dark Soft:** `0 12px 36px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.28)`

Avoid hard shadows and heavy drop depth. Premium interfaces should feel composed, not lifted.

## Color Usage Rules

- Text must always favor legibility over palette purity.
- One accent color is enough for most screens.
- Background and surface contrasts should be subtle, not high-chroma.
- Product photography should not be tinted by aggressive overlays.
- Dark mode must preserve the same hierarchy as light mode.
