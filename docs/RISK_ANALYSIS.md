# Risk Analysis

## Risk 1: Looking Like a Template

- **Impact:** Weak differentiation and lower trust.
- **Mitigation:** Use a custom visual system, editorial spacing, and carefully composed layouts.

## Risk 2: Overloading the Site with Product Noise

- **Impact:** Confused users and lower conversion.
- **Mitigation:** Organize by buyer intent and solution context rather than raw catalog density.

## Risk 3: Slow Performance from Heavy Media or Motion

- **Impact:** Lower Lighthouse scores and poorer mobile usability.
- **Mitigation:** Use optimized media, restrained animation, and server-first rendering.

## Risk 4: Weak SEO Due to Thin Content

- **Impact:** Limited organic reach.
- **Mitigation:** Build meaningful category, solution, and support pages with strong internal linking.

## Risk 5: Low Trust for International Buyers

- **Impact:** Fewer high-value enquiries.
- **Mitigation:** Surface export confidence, process clarity, and professional contact handling.

## Risk 6: Inconsistent Content Tone

- **Impact:** Premium perception breaks down.
- **Mitigation:** Use one editorial voice and a content governance layer before production.
