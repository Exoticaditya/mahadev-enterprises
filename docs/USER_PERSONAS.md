# User Personas

## 1. Studio Founder

- **Profile:** Owner of a yoga or pilates studio.
- **Goal:** Find dependable, premium products that reflect the studio brand.
- **Pain Points:** Unclear quality, inconsistent supply, and generic product presentation.
- **Conversion Trigger:** Clear product bundles, trust proof, and quick enquiry response.

## 2. Hospitality Buyer

- **Profile:** Procurement lead at a hotel, resort, or retreat centre.
- **Goal:** Source refined wellness products that elevate guest experience.
- **Pain Points:** Need for reliability, professional documentation, and visual fit.
- **Conversion Trigger:** Project suitability, export confidence, and elegant presentation.

## 3. Interior Designer / Specifier

- **Profile:** Designer selecting products for a commercial wellness space.
- **Goal:** Source items that integrate beautifully into a broader design concept.
- **Pain Points:** Lack of specification detail and weak product imagery.
- **Conversion Trigger:** Finish clarity, styling context, and professional support.

## 4. Wellness Brand Manager

- **Profile:** Marketing or operations lead for a wellness brand.
- **Goal:** Create a coherent branded experience across multiple locations.
- **Pain Points:** Need for consistency, repeatability, and premium perception.
- **Conversion Trigger:** Ability to handle multi-site or bulk orders.

## 5. International Buyer

- **Profile:** Distributor, wholesaler, or overseas studio buyer.
- **Goal:** Evaluate export-ready Indian supply partners.
- **Pain Points:** Uncertainty around communication, logistics, and trust.
- **Conversion Trigger:** Strong proof of capability and straightforward contact paths.

## Persona Design Rule

Every page should be able to answer at least one procurement question for each persona while still feeling elegant and uncluttered.
