# Product Requirements Document

## 1. Product Vision

Create a premium digital presence for Mahadev Enterprises that converts qualified visitors into enquiries while reinforcing the brand as a top-tier Indian supplier for yoga, pilates, wellness, and commercial studio solutions.

## 2. Business Objectives

- Generate high-quality enquiries from B2B and international buyers.
- Improve visibility for commercial intent search terms.
- Showcase product depth, craftsmanship, and trust signals.
- Support conversations with studios, hotels, resorts, retreat centres, and designers.
- Position the brand above commodity suppliers through editorial design and clear proof of quality.

## 3. Success Metrics

- Enquiry conversion rate from product and service pages.
- Organic traffic growth for product and location-based queries.
- Click-through rate from Google results to core landing pages.
- Engagement depth on collection, solution, and about pages.
- Mobile performance, accessibility, and Core Web Vitals outcomes.

## 4. Primary User Needs

- Understand product range quickly.
- Verify quality, reliability, and export readiness.
- Compare solutions for studio, hospitality, or retail use.
- Request pricing, catalogues, samples, or consultations.
- Trust that the supplier can handle premium or commercial projects.

## 5. Product Scope

### In Scope

- Premium marketing website with lead generation intent.
- Product category and solution pages.
- Brand story, credibility, and service positioning.
- Contact and enquiry flows.
- SEO content architecture.
- Mobile-first responsive design.
- Dark mode support.
- Motion design and refined interactions.

### Out of Scope for Phase 1

- E-commerce checkout.
- User accounts and order management.
- Complex quote configuration engine.
- ERP or warehouse integrations.
- Multi-vendor marketplace features.

## 6. Functional Requirements

- Clear navigation for product categories and business use cases.
- Dedicated enquiry pathways on every high-intent page.
- Fast-loading product presentation with imagery and key attributes.
- Trust modules for exports, manufacturing, certifications, and client types.
- Content areas for industries served and custom project support.
- SEO-ready metadata structure for all pages.

## 7. Non-Functional Requirements

- Lighthouse targets of 98+ overall with 100 accessibility, SEO, and best practices.
- Server-rendered or server-component-first architecture.
- Optimized images, lazy loading, and minimal client-side overhead.
- Strong typography hierarchy and spacing discipline.
- Frictionless performance on mobile networks.

## 8. Constraints

- No cluttered or template-like layouts.
- No overly decorative visuals that compromise trust.
- No heavy animation that harms usability or speed.
- No generic brochure language.

## 9. Definition of Done

The project is ready for development when strategy, information architecture, page hierarchy, design system principles, SEO roadmap, technical stack, and delivery plan are aligned and approved.
