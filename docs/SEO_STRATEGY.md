# SEO Strategy

## SEO Objective

Rank for high-intent commercial and product-led queries while reinforcing brand authority around premium yoga, pilates, and wellness supply in India.

## Keyword Clusters

- Yoga props supplier
- Pilates equipment supplier
- Wellness product supplier
- Commercial studio solutions
- Premium yoga accessories India
- Rishikesh wellness supplier
- Export-ready wellness products
- Hotel and resort wellness procurement

## Page-Level SEO Approach

- Build dedicated landing pages for each product category.
- Build solution pages for each buyer segment.
- Create long-form supporting content for common procurement questions.
- Use internal linking to connect product, solution, and trust pages.

## Technical SEO Priorities

- Clean information architecture.
- Fast rendering and image optimization.
- Descriptive metadata and open graph coverage.
- Structured data where relevant.
- Indexable, canonical, and crawl-friendly content.
- Strong heading structure and semantic markup.

## Local and International SEO

- Highlight Rishikesh as the brand base.
- Support India-wide commercial intent.
- Add export-friendly content for international buyers.
- Include shipping, order, and enquiry clarity for overseas evaluation.

## Content Strategy

- Educational pages for buyer questions.
- Comparison pages for product and use-case decisions.
- FAQ sections to capture long-tail searches.
- Journal articles that support trust and discovery.

## SEO Success Criteria

The site should attract qualified traffic, not just volume. Rankings matter only if they produce meaningful enquiries and sales conversations.
