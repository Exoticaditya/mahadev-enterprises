# Future Scalability Plan

## Phase 2 Opportunities

- Expand product depth with richer collections and comparison views.
- Add project case studies for higher-trust storytelling.
- Introduce downloadable catalogues or line sheets.
- Build region or use-case landing pages for SEO expansion.

## Phase 3 Opportunities

- Add a curated resources or journal system.
- Introduce multilingual support if export demand grows.
- Add CRM automation for enquiry routing and lead scoring.
- Offer more structured quote workflows for larger commercial accounts.

## Long-Term Opportunities

- Integrate a trade portal or wholesale experience if needed.
- Build a content engine around studio design, wellness procurement, and product education.
- Expand into market-specific landing pages for hotels, resorts, and premium fit-outs.

## Scalability Principle

The system should remain editorial and performant even as the catalogue grows. New content should feel like part of a coherent premium brand, not a disconnected expansion.
